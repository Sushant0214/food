Ingredients

  • 500g chicken breast, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 2 large eggs, beaten
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/3 cup pure honey
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon cooking oil (for the pan)
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Season the chicken pieces evenly with a pinch of salt and black pepper.
  2. Dip each piece of chicken first into the beaten eggs, then coat thoroughly in flour.
  3. Heat the pan with cooking oil over medium-high heat.
  4. Cook the coated chicken pieces in the pan until golden brown and fully cooked through.
  5. Drizzle the honey and soy sauce directly over the cooked chicken in the pan, tossing gently until sticky and caramelized.
  6. Remove from heat, serve warm, and enjoy your meal!
